About:
Gate to Godhood is a passion project created as a table top skirmish game and a universe to explore with other game systems like Dungeon and Dragons. Golgoth is a dark fantasy themed universe ruled by 12 Gods, benevolent and molevolent alike. Champions from around the realms come to the Bloodbound Coliseum to prove their worth to their pantheons for a chance to sip from the Chalice of Ascension to ascend to the height of demi-godhood.
