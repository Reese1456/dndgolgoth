Lore Book

Golgoth: Gate to Godhood is set in a dark fantasy universe where 12 Gods once defeated the mighty Demon Lord Golgoth and fashion a great arena from his bones. Champions from each pantheon are drawn to what is known as the Bloodbound Coliseum to prove their worth. If victorious, the champions are offered a sip from the Chalice of Ascension, allowing them to receive god-like powers to fight on behalf of their gods. However, it comes with great risk. Are you willing to take that risk?

Trial by Blood

The adventurers arrived at the Bloodbound Coliseum, where fate bound them together in battle. Randomly thrown into a warband, they were forced to fight side by side to survive the brutal trials ahead.
Their first challenge came swiftly—the Silverblade Warband, a ruthless and disciplined group of warriors, stood in their way. The battle was intense, steel clashing against steel, magic surging through the air. Just as the fight reached its peak, the Coliseum Masters unleashed the Obsidian Devourer, a monstrous construct of jagged obsidian and molten fury, throwing both sides into chaos.

Forced to battle not just each other, but the monstrous beast that threatened to consume them all, the two warbands fought desperately to survive. The Silverblades, already battered from the adventurers' relentless assault, fell first, leaving only the players standing against the rampaging Devourer. Through sheer determination, tactical skill, and unwavering resolve, the adventurers stood their ground against the titan, avoiding its magma-fueled rage and striking with precision. With one final, decisive blow, the Obsidian Devourer fell, its death throes shaking the very ground beneath them. Now, led from the arena, the victors have been escorted to the rest chambers—a rare respite in a realm that knows only battle. As they tend to their wounds and gather their strength, one question lingers:

What deadly challenge awaits them next?

About:
Gate to Godhood is a passion project created as a table top skirmish game and a universe to explore with other game systems like Dungeon and Dragons. Golgoth is a dark fantasy themed universe ruled by 12 Gods, benevolent and molevolent alike. Champions from around the realms come to the Bloodbound Coliseum to prove their worth to their pantheons for a chance to sip from the Chalice of Ascension to ascend to the height of demi-godhood.
